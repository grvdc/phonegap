Definitions

Single Family

Occupied by one family group or extended family.

Duplex

Housing divided into two sections, or housing each having a separate entrance.

Apartment Building

Residential housing with common entrance areas.

Multi Family Low Rise

Apartment Buildings not more than 3 stories.

Multi Family High Rise

Apartment Buildings above 3 stories.

Gated Community

Housing where access is restricted by a common gate and walls for security and privacy.

Retirement Community

Housing reserved for older adults still having the ability for self-care.

Student Housing

Cooperative housing for students.

Public Housing

Federal, State or locally funded in part or wholly by tax payers for low income families.
