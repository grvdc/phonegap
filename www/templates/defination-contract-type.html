Definitions

Lump Sum

Price is fixed for a set of documents.

Cost Plus % Contract

Contractor earns a percentage of the total spent on the project.

Cost Plus % fee, not to Exceed

Two-tiered approach, percentage of the spent on the project but the fee cannot exceed a specified amount.

Future

A system where the builder provides both the design and construction work. This can be coupled with, Lump Sum, Cost Plus %, Cost Plus %fee and not to exceed

Sub Contract

The contractor does not have a direct relationship to the owner.

Insurance Agreement/Unit Pricing

Unit pricing provided by the contractor at the time of the bid. Administrative costs are part of the pricing, generally with % of the contract for profit.
